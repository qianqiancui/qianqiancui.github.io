Bias in AI Project

  • Main Hypothesis: AI facial recognition techniques would perpetuate the human bias in social categorization - hypodescent would be produced by the AI techniques when recognizing racially ambiguous faces, and the amount of hypodescent would depend on the racial composition of the training dataset

  • Tested a popular convolutional neural network ResNet and fine-tuned the network with multiple public face datasets (unbalanced in terms of race and gender)
  • Implemented a Siamese network that aims at comparing similarity of the two faces
  • Generated 200+ morphed faces with WebMorph to test the network’s performance
  • Wrote python scripts able to only include the oval shaped facial areas within 2 minutes for 200+ faces
