Creativity

Independent Projects

iOS app: Habit Tracker With an interest in tracking my own habits and a love for space, I am working on a space themed iOS productivity app.
Users will play the role of an astronaut. They are encouraged to set up goals and record time they spend on those goals. A star will light up as a mental reward when the users spend every ten hours on one habit.
I independently developed graphic design with Photoshop, created 3D models with Blender and programmed in Swift. I’m currently making some modifications on this app and will bring it to the iOS App Store in the future. (An animated example: when a user gets a star)

   

Illustrations

I learned to draw by myself. To me, drawing is a way of intuitive thinking and an approach to inform my research. Anime such as Ghost in the Shell (1995 film) gave me a lot of inspiration. Eastern aesthetics influence my artistic style.
Being born and raised in a traditional Asian family, I got confused by my lesbian identity for a long time and struggled with the burden of cultural expectations for women. With my pencil, I hope to explore the themes of women, identity, freedom and future.


















(I draw this digital illustration for the missing women of China due to postnatal and prenatal sex selection. I am motivated to promote gender equality and minority rights with my research.)
