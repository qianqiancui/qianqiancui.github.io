Biography

My name is Qianqian (Lee) Cui. I am currently a MA student in NYU’s General Psychology program, working on my thesis under the guidance of Dr. David M. Amodio in the Amodio Social Neuroscience Lab.
I am interested in how social cognitive knowledge influences social perception and attitude formation. I am also open to other related topics and passionate about developing prejudice interventions. Please see my research here.
During my downtime, I love to draw digital illustrations as an approach to inform my research. I am also working on an iOS habit tracker app independently. [Curriculum Vitae]
